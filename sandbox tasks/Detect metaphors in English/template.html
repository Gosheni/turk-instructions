You will be given a verb and a sentence that contains the given verb. You will be asked to rate whether the verb is used in a literal sense or a metaphorical sense in that sentence.

Below are some typical properties of metaphorical and literal senses:  

Literal usages tend to be:
- more basic, straightforward meaning; more physical, closely tied to our senses: vision, hearing, touching, tasting

Metaphorical usages tend to be:
- more complex; more distant from our senses; more abstract; more vague; often surprising; tend to bring in imagery from a different domain
