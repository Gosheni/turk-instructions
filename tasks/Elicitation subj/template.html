What can do this action?

  • Watch a short clip from YouTube of an actor performing an action (requires Flash).
  • First, give a word that describes what is happening in the video.  Don't be too specific for the action; if you want to use more than one word, you're being too specific.
  • Next, in the blanks below the video, supply at least one or two things, people, or animals that can perform the action.  Try to be specific and creative here; writing "man" or "person" for everything will get your responses rejected.
  • As you give your responses, a text box will fill in with an example sentence with your action and the thing doing that action.  It's okay if the grammar doesn't work out correctly; it's just there to guide you.
  • In order to make sure that we get good data, you will need to match the answer of at least one other participant in order to get credit for this HIT.  So, it is in your interest to to give more answers rather than fewer and to try to "match" what other people would say.  (If you've ever watched Family Feud, it's like that.)
  • For example, if you saw a movie of someone dunking a basketball, you would write "dunking" as the action and then "basketball player", "michael jordan", "shaq", "athelete", "forward", etc. for what can do that action.
  • You can use "TAB" to move between text boxes without clicking.
  • We are interested in what can do the action; do not confuse it with what you would do the action to.  For example, a "husband" can give a "gift", but a "gift" isn't doing the giving.
