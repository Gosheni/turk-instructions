Survey Instructions

Before answering the survey below please complete the following steps:

  1. Decide on a topic you would like to write a pretend "Fake News" article on, and write it down in the first textbox below marked "Topic". Please note the "Fake Article" you will generate will not be published anywhere, it will only be used to gauge the potential threat current systems hold.
  2. Navigate to this page.
  3. Use the generator there to write a Fake News article. You can adjust different metadata such as the headline, the website, and the date and then click "Generate" next to the "Article" section to generate an article. You can also edit the article and have the system attempt to find the correct metadata for it.
  4. Once you are satisfied with the article you have written, please use the blue button next to the header that reads "Fake Article" to copy the text, and paste in the in the second textbox below marked "Article".
  5. Finally, complete the survey below about your experience. There is a textbox at the end for comments, should you have any.

Please note: The survey below is a general survey that a number of teams with wildly different systems are being asked to fill-out, so the questions themselves may seem vague. We have provided some system-specific guidance where necessary.
