Overview

For this task, we would like you to sign up for an account for the game Know Your Nyms? and play 25 rounds of the game (each round takes  5-20 seconds).

Steps

  1. Go to www.know-your-nyms.com and click the 'Start Round' button.
  2. You will be prompted to sign in. Sign in using Username: ${Username} and Password: ${Password}
  3. You are now ready to play the game. Please review the rules and tips below, and then click the 'Start Round' button on the homepage.
  4. At the end of each round, click the 'Next Round' button.
  5. After playing 25 rounds, submit your username below. You will be paid once we validate that your username has played 25 rounds.  You can check how many rounds you have played by going to the home screen and looking at the Total Rounds Played.

Rules & Tips

  • When you start a round, you will be provided a question such as "What are the parts of a car?" You then have 20 seconds during which to enter as many parts of a car as you can think of. 
  • At the end of 20 seconds, you will be brought to a scoring page
  • To add a new entry, either press the 'Enter' key or the 'tab' key on your keyboard.
  • Different forms of the same word (such as 'tire' and 'tires') will not be double-counted. 

Examples

Example Question: “What are the parts of a laptop?”
Example  Answers:

  • screen
  • keyboard
  • key
  • mouse pad
  • speaker
  • port
  • chip

You can check how many rounds you have played by going to the home screen and looking at the Total Rounds Played.
