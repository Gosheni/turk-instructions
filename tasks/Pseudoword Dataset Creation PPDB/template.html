We are researchers from the University of Pennsylvania, trying to help computers understand language! Sometimes two words can mean the same thing in one sentence, but not in another. Teaching computers to understand the difference allows them to do useful things for us like answer our questions or summarize news articles.
 
Please read the instructions carefully, EVEN IF YOU HAVE DONE HITS FOR US BEFORE. There are details that are specific to this task.

 

Please check all substitutes that could replace the blue word in the sentence shown, using your best judgement. The substitute should retain the meaning of the original sentence and make sense. Most sentences will have just one substitute to choose from, but some have more. If none of the substitutes listed are good replacements for the blue word in the sentence, select NONE.

These sentences have been 'pre-processed' by converting all letters to lowercase, separating punctuation, and splitting conjunctions. Please ignore this in your work and do not allow it to affect your judgement of whether a paraphrases is a good substitute for the blue word. 

It is also okay if the paraphrased sentence is contains small grammatical errors, as long as they do not drastically alter the meaning. E.g. the following is a good substitute:

The boy is running.

The boys is running.

but the following is a bad substitute:

I am running.

You are running.

It is okay if the paraphrased sentence contains more/less detail than the original sentence, as long as it does not drastically alter the meaning. E.g. the following is a good substitute:

The person is running.

The boy is running.

but the following is a bad substitute:

The cells undergo development.

The cells undergo economic development.

The substitute should make sense in the particular sentence shown. E.g. the following is a bad substitute, even though center might be a good substitute for heart in some other sentences.

The heart is an organ.

The center is an organ.

These HITs will be quality controlled. We will have to reject any HITs with questions left unanswered. Please ensure you mark at least one response to every question.

Thank you in advance!
