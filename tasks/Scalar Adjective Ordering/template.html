This HIT asks you determine the relative intensity of adjectives.

You will be provided with a set of adjectives that describe the same attribute (e.g. warm, hot, and scalding all describe temperature). The adjectives can be placed along an intensity scale, from less intense to more intense (warm < hot < scalding). For each pair of adjectives (word1, word2) within the set, we want you to tell us which of the following is true:

  • word1 is less intense than word2 (word1 < word2)
  • word2 is less intense than word1 (word2 < word1), or
  • word1 and word2 are equally intense (word1 = word2)

Here are some examples with a correct answer selected in each row.
