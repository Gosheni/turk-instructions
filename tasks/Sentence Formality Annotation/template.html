We are performing a research study on writing style. Please read the following sentences and determine the formality of each sentence. In general, we consider formal language to the type of language that is appropriate for professional and business conmmunication. However, there is no perfect definition of formality, so use your own experience and best judgement to make your decisions, keeping in mind the following guidelines:

  • The formality of a sentence should not necessarily be dictated by the relationship between the people involved in the communication. I.e. it is possible for an employee to send speak informally to their boss. 
  • The formality of a sentence should not necessarily be dictated by the personal/business nature of the sentence. I.e. it is possible for a person to speak/write informally about work/business or to write formally about personal matters.
  • If the sentence is blank, not in English, or otherwise uninterpretable, please choose I cannot tell.

Also, please indicate using the check box if you believe the sentence was not part of a person-to-person communication, e.g. if it appears to be spam or promotional. You should still rate the formality of the text, even if you check this checkbox. 

These HITs will be quality controlled. If you are not sure whether you are doing them correctly, please do a small number and wait for feedback before doing more. Thank you in advance!

 

NOTE: We acknowledge that some of the messages that are show are quite lengthy. We understand this and we do not expect you to read the entire post in great depth. But please do keep in mind the goal of the task is to rate the formality of the text. Please do not just read the first few sentences. Scan the entire message so that you can get an accurate assessment of the overall tone. Thank you!

Examples of very informal sentences (should receive a rating of 1 or 2):
