Overview

Thanks for participating in this HIT!

In this HIT, you'll be given an image and 4 candidate sentences. Your task is to select the best sentence describing the image.

Note:
  • Please be forgiving of minor spelling errors.
  • There might be more than one statement (or None) that matches the image. Try to do your best to choose the most plausible and sensible option.
  • If you are not sure about your answer for the above reasons, you can check the "not clear" box
