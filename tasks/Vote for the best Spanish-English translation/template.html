Choose the best translation

The two groups of sentences below have been translated from Spanish into English. The first group is from a news article, while the second is from a dialogue between two speakers. Many of the translations were produced by non-native English speakers, and their translations are often ungrammatical, misspelled, disfluent, or bad in other ways.  However, many of them are quite reasonable and clear. Choose the sentence that you feel is most natural in context.

You should consider the following factors when selecting one translation as the best:

  • Does it make more sense than the others?
  • Is the English reasonably good?
  • Do the grammar and spelling require only minimal correction?
