We are performing a research study on writing style. Please read the following words and rate the formality of each. In general, we consider formal words to be the type of words that are appropriate for professional and business conmmunication. However, there is no perfect definition of formality, so use your own experience and best judgement to make your decisions, keeping in mind the following guidelines:

  • The formality of a word should not necessarily be dictated by the relationship between the people involved in the communication. I.e. it is possible for an employee to send speak informally to their boss. 
  • The formality of a word should not necessarily be dictated by the personal/business nature of the word. I.e. it is possible for a person to speak/write informally about professional topics or to speak/write formally about personal topics.
  • If the word is blank, not in English, or otherwise uninterpretable, please choose I cannot tell.

These HITs will be quality controlled. If you are not sure whether you are doing them correctly, please do a small number and wait for feedback before doing more. Thank you in advance!

 

Some examples of formal words (should receive a rating of 6 or 7): disservice, credentials, suffice, therein, withhold, insufficient

Some examples of informal words (should receive a rating of 1 or 2): hey, cool, :-), lol, awesome, jerk
