Order 2 steps given a goal

In this task, we ask you to order 2 steps given a goal. We will provide a goal, and 2 actions that are steps to accomplish this goal. Your task is to choose which of these 2 steps you think should be done first.

  • Answer according to your common sense, but not necessarily what you would personally do.
  • If the steps or goals are hard to understand, do your best to answer.
  • You are encouraged to look up words you don't understand.

Reason: It's obvious that you have to provide the basic facts to draft a report before you submit it.
